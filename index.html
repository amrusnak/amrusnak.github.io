Design

Future forward design takes vision and courage. Instead of formulaic work that blends in, Alex can bring a fresh and exciting aesthetic to your brand. His style is informed by the digital world, swiss design, and art nouveau. In a world saturated with the same tired design tropes, it's crucial to stick out.

Code

Good software has the power to chainge the world. Alex's coding experience goes well beyond the front end. He has studied algorithmic structure to understand truly efficient implementation. With experience ranging from web code to blockchain validification to machine learning models, he has the ability to work on diverse projects.

Art

Art provides an avenue to explore the human condition. Alex is a prolific painter with a deep interest in bringing the philosophical questions that have perplexed humans for centuries into a modern context. His practice is focused on public art to reach the largest audience for his ideas.

Biography

Alex was born in Baltimore, Maryland and received his primary education at the Sacred Heart School and Zurich International School. After returning from abroad, he attended Loyola Blakefield Preparatory School for secondary education. He will graduate in May of 2018 from the University of South Carolina with a B.F.A. in graphic design and minor in computer science. Among his notable achievements while in college are: working on $46,000 worth of public art grants, internships at CyberChain and the Richland County Public Library, developing an applied computing learning community with UofSC faculty, and creating a winning program at the Queen City Data Science Hackathon.
